Biography

Problem solving has been a theme throughout my career and hobbies. I have always been fascinated by the challenge of solving puzzles. I love coding because it represents the opportunity to apply logic and creativity to solve problems while indulging my preference for detail orientation.

The love of problem solving has informed my fields of study. While studying engineering, my favourite subject by far was mathematics. The use of logic to solve problems shaped my relationship to the subjects as if it was one big, intricate puzzle to solve.

After jewellery studies I concentrated on bringing my own designs to life. My interest was always captured by mechanical and kinetic elements to create jewellery that does more than sparkle. Jewellery pieces that move, can change or transform has been a years long obsession. These are complex puzzles to solve, requiring meticulous planning and a dedication to getting the details perfect.
